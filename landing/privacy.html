Privacy Policy

This policy will take effect when the app launches. It describes how the app is built to handle your data.

Your fridge photos

Photos you scan are uploaded securely, processed to identify ingredients, and automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours. We keep only the resulting ingredient list. Photos are never used to train any models and are never shared with anyone.

What we store

  • Your account (Sign in with Apple) and a display handle.
  • Your dietary preferences and allergies, used only to generate recipes for you.
  • Ingredient lists from your scans and recipes you save, cook, rate, or publish.
  • Basic usage events that help us improve the app.

What we never do

  • Sell your data.
  • Use your photos for anything other than identifying your ingredients.
  • Track you across other apps or websites.

Subscriptions

Payments are handled entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Deleting your account

You can delete your account and all associated data from inside the app under Settings. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Contact

Questions? Reach us through the support page.
