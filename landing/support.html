Support

We actually read these.

Email

For help, feedback, or recipe complaints (fair), email edgardorubero@gmail.com. We aim to reply within 2 business days.

Common fixes

  • Scan missed something: add it by hand from the ingredient list. Tap the plus button.
  • Wrong dietary results: check your preferences under Settings. They apply to every generation.
  • Subscription issues: purchases and refunds are handled by Apple through your App Store account. Use Restore Purchases in Settings after reinstalling.

Delete your account

Open the app, go to Settings, then Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and data.
