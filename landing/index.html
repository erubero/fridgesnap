Questions

Does it work with any kitchen?

Yes. Fridge, pantry, counter, that one drawer. Photograph whatever you have and it works with it. Recipes may assume you own salt, pepper, and cooking oil, and we ask you once to confirm that.

What if it misses an ingredient?

Photo recognition is good, not psychic. Anything it is unsure about gets flagged so you can confirm or remove it, and you can add items by hand in one tap.

Are the calories accurate?

They are estimates, clearly labeled as estimates. Good enough to keep you honest, not a medical device.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, in two taps, through your App Store subscriptions. The trial is 7 days and you will not be charged if you cancel before it ends.

Is my data private?

Your fridge photos are processed to identify ingredients and deleted within 24 hours. They are never used for training and never shared. See the privacy policy.
