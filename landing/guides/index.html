Guides: Cooking From What You Already Own

Everything we know about turning a random fridge into dinner, written down.

FridgeSnap generates recipes from a photo of your fridge, but the thinking behind it works even without the app. These guides cover that thinking: how to see meals in random ingredients, how to keep food alive longer, and how to stop feeding your trash can.

The guides

  • What can I make with the ingredients in my fridge? The four-slot dinner formula that turns random ingredients into actual meals.
  • How to get recipes from a photo of your fridge. How photo scanning works, how to take a photo that scans well, and what happens to your pictures.
  • How to use food before it goes bad. The fridge triage order, storage tricks that buy extra days, and rescue meals.
  • Easy recipes using ingredients you already have. Seven forgiving recipe templates, sorted from barely conscious to actually trying.
  • How to reduce food waste at home. Five habits that keep food out of the trash and money in your pocket.
